Lowkey

a web application designed on Affinity Designer and developed in Symfony and Vue.js

During my 2nd year of a Computer Science degree, I had to create a web application using Symfony for the back-end and Vue.js for the front-end. The aim was to create an API with Symfony and link the SQL database with the Vue.js front-end, enabling a dynamic application.

Lowkey was a music culture medium, showcasing artists of varying degrees of fame and explaining the behind-the-scenes in order to better understand music. This site was intended to give them a platform for showcasing their content and listing all their previous work.

Unfortunately, the medium became inactive not long after the end of my second year, so the site was never officially posted.

Thanks to Lowkey and its three members for giving me the opportunity to work on their project. Thanks to my teachers for supporting me throughout my end-of-year project.

La Bête

a creative website designed on Figma and developed with Vite.js & GSAP

In March 2024, two French musical artists, FEMTOGO and Vilhelm. released a joint 5-track project immersing us in a medieval and chivalric atmosphere. It's a project that has marked my musical year 2024, with a yearly top 5 composed solely of this project.

With its strong visual identity, this project also had an impact on me graphically, which is why I wanted to develop a creative website around this artistic direction imagined by icemigi.

On March 12, 2024, a teaser straight out of a FromSoftware video game appeared on the instagram of the two musical artists.

The trailer, directed by Holow, plunges us straight into the medieval ambience of the project, with a soundtrack by Vilhelm. and 3d visuals immersing us in this heroic fantasy universe.

I decided to keep the design of the site very simplistic to highlight the visuals created around the project. A background in the same color as the cover of the musical project. Underlining effects in the main colors of the cover and tracklist. And I reused the same typography as that used in the tracklist.

For each page, a sound from the project is used in the background, except for the page dedicated to the reworked version and the page dedicated to Yev.'s creation, where I used sounds from Vilhelm.'s reworked version.

For site navigation I wanted to change from a simplistic navigation bar and opted for a banner using GSAP for scrolling.

This makes navigation between pages unique and allows complete immersion in the project.

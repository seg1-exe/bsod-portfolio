Inktober 2025

a collection of small creative web applications.

Originally a challenge to boost the creativity of artists and illustrators, I reappropriated Inktober in my own way and took the opportunity to create 31 web applications related to the assigned themes.

From revisiting mini video games that have become cult classics over time, to visual and interactive experiences, to reproducing filters that you might see on social media...

This challenge has not only improved my creativity, it has also taught me discipline, given me deadlines to meet, and introduced me to new technologies to master.

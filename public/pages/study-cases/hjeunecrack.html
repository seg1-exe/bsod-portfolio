1er Mouvement

a creative website designed on Figma and developed with Vite.js

To mark the release of H JeuneCrack's 1er Mouvement project, I collaborated with skii and Claire to create a website analyzing the project. Claire's and skii's writing talents were highlighted by the visuals created for the project's identity.

Accompanied by visuals by Lena Borghesi, Holow, KHA2, Étienne Saby, Marina Maruyama, Louna Aucomte and photos by Argenteurs, this analysis allows us to discover or rediscover this project from another angle. By listening to each track while reading the short analysis, you can discover the details of each phrase.

Each track is illustrated by a photograph taken by Argenteurs, an analysis written by skii or Claire and a player for listening to the track.

As the tracks were clipped by Etienne Saby, they are illustrated by the latter, allowing you to visualize them while reading the analysis.

The simplistic effect of the site and the emphasis on visuals was successful. Reading the texts and navigating between the pieces is pleasant and makes the project easier to understand.
